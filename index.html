About Don Victor Ministries

Don Victor Ministries exists to fulfill the Great Commission by leading people to Christ and equipping believers for effective Kingdom service in alignment with Ephesians 4:12 and Matthew 28:19-20.

Show More...

Our Vision and Mission is clear and God-ordained: Founded by Rev. Don Victor, this ministry was born from a clear divine mandate to win six million souls for Christ across six continents. This isn't merely a numerical goal—it represents 6 million lives transformed, 6 million families restored, 6 million testimonies of God's faithfulness, and 6 million eternal destinies secured in Christ Jesus. Our foundation rests upon evangelism, discipleship, and fellowship.

We pursue this vision with faith, wisdom, and innovation. We ensure the Gospel reaches people where they are while remaining steadfast in truth and power. Our app and digital platforms are built on prayer to enable people across nations to interact, learn, and grow regardless of distance.

Rev. Don Victor, Ph.D

This is not just technology—it is consecrated technology. This is not just an app—it is Kingdom infrastructure. This is not just a platform—it is holy ground. Welcome to Don Victor Ministries Global. Together, we will win six million souls and change the world for Christ.
